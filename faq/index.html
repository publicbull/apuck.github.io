FAQ

Does every book have AR level information?
No. So far, the Accelerated Reader program has around 150,000 titles.
Does the level information change?
Normally no changes.
Why is my scan history gone?
Every new installation, the APP create new list for you. So, the list will be removed when you delete the app.
How can I if the app keep crash?
Our support team is more than happy to assist you. Please feel free to contact us in the app or on our website.
